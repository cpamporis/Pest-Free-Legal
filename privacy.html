Privacy Policy – Pestify

Pestify is a business-to-business mobile application, created and owned by Pest-Free, designed to support professional pest control operations, including inspections, service logging, and compliance reporting.

This application is intended exclusively for authorized business users, such as administrators, technicians, and approved customer representatives.

Data We Process

  • User identification details (name, email address, role)
  • Authentication credentials (securely hashed and encrypted where applicable)
  • Top-view photos of the property uploaded by users
  • Service logs, visit records, and compliance reports
  • Customer and location identifiers related to inspections

Purpose of Processing

  • User authentication and access control
  • Performing and documenting pest control services
  • Generating inspection and compliance reports
  • Meeting regulatory and contractual obligations
  • Ensuring system security and operational integrity

We do not use data for advertising, tracking, profiling, or marketing purposes.

Data Storage & Security

All data is stored on secure cloud infrastructure using encrypted communications (HTTPS). Access to data is restricted to authorized users within the same organization.

Account Deletion

User accounts are created and managed by company administrators. Users who wish to request deletion of their account and associated personal data may contact: info@pest-free.gr Upon identity verification, the administrator will delete the user account and associated personal data, except where retention is required by law. Deletion requests are processed within 7 business days.

Data Sharing

Personal data is not sold, rented, or shared with third parties. Infrastructure providers process data solely for hosting and technical operation.

User Rights

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their data in accordance with GDPR.

Contact: info@pest-free.gr
